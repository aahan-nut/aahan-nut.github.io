5. James Harden (2017-2020)

In his prime, nobody could guard James Harden. Literally nobody could stop this man. Whether it was the infamous step back or the rule exploitation, he was insane.

4. Kobe Bryant (2005-2010)

The Black Mamba, unstoppable. Took and greened shots over multiple defenders.

3. Michael Jordan (Mid 90s)

Regarded as the GOAT, but played in a different era and it's tough to judge how he could adjust in modern times. Still, one heck of a player with crazy athleticism.

2. Kevin Durant (2013-14 and 2017-18)

Crazy to think what could've been if injuries didn't riddle his career. The Slim Reaper, standing at 6'11, was a nightmare for defenders as he shot hangpulls at 54 percent.

1. LeBron James (2008-2018)

Don't overthink it. Just look at how long that prime was. This is the freaking GOAT, LeBron Raymone James. An unstoppable force that makes opponents hallucinate.
